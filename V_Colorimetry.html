Colorimetry

Colorimetría

  • Colorimetry quantified in 4 states of ripeness:
    • Immature (I)
    • Medium Ripe (MM)
    • Ripe (M)
    • Overripe (SM)
  • In 3 populations of Entre Ríos:
    • Concordia
    • PN El Palmar
    • Gualeguaychú
  • CieLab scale:
    • color_L: luminosity
    • +a: red
    • -a: green
    • +b: yellow
    • -b: blue
  • Instrument:
    • 3NH NH300 digital colorimeter
